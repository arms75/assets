Tuesday Upper · 8 exercises
Dumbbell Bench Press6–10 reps
  • Shoulder blades pinned back and down into the bench
  • Slight arch, feet planted, drive through the floor
  • Lower to mid-chest with elbows ~45° — not flared to 90°
  • Wrists stacked over elbows; press up and slightly together
Seated Dumbbell Overhead Press8–12 reps
  • Brace the core and keep ribs down — don’t flare the ribcage
  • Start at ear height, elbows just in front of the body
  • Press up and slightly in; stop just short of lockout
  • No lower-back arch lifting off the seat
Neutral Grip Machine-Assisted Pull-Up8–12 reps
  • Start from a full dead hang, shoulders engaged not shrugged
  • Lead with the elbows driving down to the ribs
  • Chest toward the bar — think “elbows to pockets”
  • Control the descent to a full stretch
Single Arm High Cable Lateral Raise12–20 reps
  • High pulley, working arm starts across the body
  • Soft elbow; raise out to shoulder height, lead with the elbow
  • Pinky slightly up; squeeze the side delt
  • No torso lean or swing — control it back down
Cable Face Pull12–20 reps
  • Rope at face height; pull toward the eyes/forehead
  • Elbows high, rotate so knuckles face the ceiling at the end
  • Squeeze the rear delts; don’t shrug the traps
  • Slow controlled return to a full reach
Single Arm Bayesian Curl10–15 reps
  • Low pulley behind you; arm stretched back for a big stretch
  • Upper arm stays fixed — curl from the elbow only
  • Hard squeeze at the top, full stretch at the bottom
  • No body swing or shoulder drifting forward
Single Arm Neutral Grip Cable Triceps Pushdown10–15 reps
  • Elbow tucked and pinned to your side throughout
  • Neutral grip; extend fully and squeeze at lockout
  • Only the forearm moves — upper arm stays still
  • Control the return to a full stretch
Bent-Over Underhand Grip Barbell Row6–10 reps
  • Hinge to ~45° with a flat back and braced core
  • Underhand grip; pull the bar to the lower ribs/belly
  • Lead with the elbows, squeeze the mid-back
  • Don’t jerk with the lower back or stand up as you pull
Thursday Lower · 5 exercises
Pin-Loaded Quad-Biased Leg Press8–12 reps
  • Feet lower and ~shoulder-width for quad bias
  • Lower under control to a deep but safe knee bend
  • Keep the lower back flat on the pad — no rounding at the bottom
  • Press through mid-foot; don’t snap the knees into lockout
Barbell Romanian Deadlift8–12 reps
  • Soft knees; push the hips back — hinge, don’t squat
  • Bar stays close to the legs, flat back throughout
  • Lower until you feel the hamstring stretch (~mid-shin)
  • Drive the hips forward to stand; squeeze glutes at the top
Plate-Loaded Machine Hip Thrust8–12 reps
  • Shoulder blades on the pad, chin tucked, starting from the top
  • Ribs down, posterior tilt — don’t overarch the back
  • Drive through the heels to a full glute squeeze
  • Control the descent; knees track over the toes
Leg Extension10–15 reps
  • Back flat to the pad, knee joint aligned with the pivot
  • Extend fully and squeeze the quads at the top
  • Control the lower — don’t let the stack slam
  • Keep hips down; don’t jerk off the seat
Seated Hamstring Curl10–15 reps
  • Pad just above the heels, thighs secured
  • Curl fully and squeeze; keep the hips down
  • Control the return to a full stretch
  • Consistent foot position each rep
Saturday Upper · 7 exercises
Underhand Close Grip Cable Lat Pulldown8–12 reps
  • Underhand ~shoulder-width grip; chest tall, slight lean back
  • Pull to the upper chest, elbows down and back
  • Lead with the elbows; squeeze the lats at the bottom
  • Control up to a full stretch — don’t over-lean
Pin-Loaded Machine Chest Press8–12 reps
  • Seat set so the handles sit at mid-chest
  • Shoulder blades back and down against the pad
  • Press out and slightly together; don’t roll the shoulders forward
  • Stop just short of lockout; control the return
Single Arm Low-To-High Machine Low Row8–12 reps
  • Set up tall and brace against rotating the torso
  • Drive the elbow back and slightly up; squeeze the mid-back
  • Keep the shoulder down — no shrug
  • Full stretch forward each rep without twisting the spine
Cable Reverse Curl10–15 reps
  • Overhand (pronated) grip; elbows pinned to your sides
  • Curl up keeping the wrists firm and straight
  • Squeeze at the top, control to a full stretch
  • No swinging — only the forearms move
Cable Straight Bar Overhead Triceps Extension10–15 reps
  • Face away, bar overhead, upper arms beside the ears
  • Keep the elbows in and pointing forward — don’t flare
  • Extend fully and squeeze; only the forearms move
  • Control the stretch behind the head; brace the core
Cable Face Pull12–20 reps
  • Pull to the eyes; elbows high
  • Rotate so knuckles finish toward the ceiling
  • Squeeze rear delts; no trap shrug
  • Slow controlled return
Standing Dumbbell Lateral Raise12–20 reps
  • Slight forward lean, soft elbows, lead with the elbows
  • Raise to shoulder height — “pour the jug”, pinky slightly up
  • No swinging or shrugging the traps up
  • Control the lower for constant tension
Sunday Lower · 7 exercises
Sumo Deadlift6–10 reps
  • Wide stance, toes out, hands grip inside the knees
  • Hips down, chest up, flat back; take the slack out first
  • Push the floor away, knees out, bar dragging close
  • Lock out with the hips/glutes — don’t hyperextend the back
Goblet Squat8–15 reps
  • Dumbbell at the chest, elbows tucked in
  • Brace and sit between the hips; knees track over the toes
  • Depth to ~parallel or just below, heels down
  • Drive up through mid-foot; chest stays tall
Standing Machine Glute Kickback10–15 reps
  • Hips square to the pad, slight forward lean
  • Drive from the glute, not the lower back
  • Full squeeze at the top; don’t arch the spine
  • Control the return; movement stays at the hip
Seated Machine Hip Abduction10–15 reps
  • Sit tall — a slight forward lean hits the glutes more
  • Push the knees out under control, full range
  • Squeeze the glutes at the end position
  • Slow the return — don’t let the pads snap back
Pin-Loaded Leg Press Calf Raise10–15 reps
  • Balls of the feet on the edge, heels free
  • Full stretch at the bottom, press to a high contraction
  • Brief pause at the top — no bouncing
  • Knees mostly fixed; drive through the calves
Single Arm High Cable Lateral Raise12–20 reps
  • High pulley, working arm across the body
  • Soft elbow; raise to shoulder height, lead with the elbow
  • Pinky slightly up; squeeze the side delt
  • No lean or swing — control it back
Machine Crunch (Overhead Handles)10–15 reps
  • Grip the handles but flex through the abs, don’t pull with the arms
  • Round the spine — ribs toward the pelvis
  • Control the return; don’t let it yank you back up
  • Steady tempo, exhale as you crunch
Cues are general technique reminders to pair with the 9 Upper/Lower program — not a substitute for a coach’s eyes on your individual form. Adjust ranges of motion to what your joints tolerate comfortably.
