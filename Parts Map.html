How to use this

Pick a recent moment with some charge to it — a snippy reply, a wave of dread, the urge to clean instead of doing the thing. Write it down, then map the parts that were active. For each one: where you feel it in the body, what it's trying to do for you, how old it feels, how long it's been on the job.

The pivotal step is the Self-check: how do I feel toward this part? Curiosity, warmth, or neutral openness means you're in Self. Anything else — irritation, fear, "this part is the problem" — means another part has blended in. That's not a failure; it's another member of the system. Note it.

You're building a map, not healing anything in one pass. The regulars panel below surfaces parts that recur across sessions — that recognition is most of the value, and it compounds.

The boundary: if mapping a protector opens a younger, rawer feeling faster than you want, stop. Tick the exile flag, leave it closed, and bring that to a trained practitioner rather than pushing in alone.
