Rizatriptan Tracker

Tracking treatment days per month — the number that matters for medication-overuse risk.

Why days, not doses: medication-overuse headache risk is counted in days used per month, not total tablets. For triptans the commonly cited threshold is 10 or more treatment days per month, sustained over 3+ months. Two tablets on one day is still one treatment day. This is a personal log to spot trends and inform a conversation with your doctor — not medical advice or a dosing guide.
