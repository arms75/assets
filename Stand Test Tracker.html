Stand Test Tracker

Lying-to-standing orthostatic log — heart rate & blood pressure across timepoints, for discussion with a clinician.

Before you start: Don’t do this alone — have someone with you. Stand near a wall or corner for support. The moment you feel genuinely faint or grey-out, stop and lie back down. Reproducing mild symptoms is informative; an actual faint is not worth it. Do it well-hydrated, having eaten normally, and not straight after a hot night. This log is a record to bring to a doctor — it is not a diagnosis.
