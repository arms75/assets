Wash-Day Routine

Curl Care · Weekly

Fine 3A · two wash days + refresh
Sun
Main wash
Cleanse · Treat · Style
  1. Cleanse with Noughty Wave Hello, scalp only — suds rinse through the lengths. Every 1–2 wks swap in Joico clarifying when curls feel coated, limp or stop clumping.
  2. Treat — alternate week to week:
    Wk A · Moisture — Curlsmith mask, 10–15 min. Skip the conditioner.
    Wk B · Strength — Curlsmith bond repair, then a little Umberto Frizz Fix for slip.
  3. Style with Wavy curl crème on soaking-wet hair. Scrunch, then diffuse or air-dry.
Wed
Mid-week refresh
Gentle
  1. Cleanse gently with the Wavy cowash, scalp focus.
  2. A little Umberto Frizz Fix on the ends for slip.
  3. Restyle with the curl crème on wet hair.
Daily
Between washes
Protect & revive
  • Pineapple (loose high pony) at night + satin / silk pillowcase.
  • Revive day 2–3: mist with water, scrunch in a pea-size of curl crème.

Keep an eye on

Buildup before dryness. The cowash and crème both build up, so when curls go flat or stringy, clarify — don't add more product.

Bond ↔ moisture balance. Alternating by week keeps them in check. If your Curlsmith mask is a protein / repair one, don't run it the same week as the bond treatment. Stiff or straw-like hair = protein overload → lean into moisture and pause the bond product for a few weeks.
