When curls go flat or stringy, that's buildup — not dryness. Clarify; don't pile on more product.

Weekly treatment · alternate

  • Wk A · Moisture — Curlsmith mask, 10–15 min. Skip the conditioner.
  • Wk B · Strength — Curlsmith bond repair, then a little Umberto Frizz Fix for slip.

Between washes

  • Pineapple (loose high pony) at night + a satin / silk pillowcase.
  • Revive day 2–3: mist with water, scrunch in a pea-size of curl crème.

Bond ↔ moisture balance

  • Alternating week to week is what keeps the two in check.
  • Don't run a protein / repair mask the same week as the bond treatment.
  • Stiff or straw-like hair = protein overload → lean into moisture, pause the bond product for a few weeks.
