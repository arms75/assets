What changed, and why

The novel is now the daily spine: six sessions a week (Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Sat, Sun), one fixed rest day. Same evening cue on weekdays — after dinner, 18:30 — because a fixed time-and-place cue is the single strongest predictor of a habit sticking. Wednesday is deliberately short (45 min after the second job); Saturday morning is the long, fresh-brain session with the lecture stacked directly in front of it.

Friday is a real rest day. Planned rest isn't a broken chain — it's what prevents the missed-day guilt spiral. Everywhere else, the rule is never miss twice.

SQL is untouched: Mon/Tue 16:30, Sat 11:15, Sun optional catch-up — exactly as before.

Paused for draft season: drawing (it shared the writing slot — one creative focus until THE END) and the Thursday Curiosity Rotation (Sunday's session stays). Blender keeps its Saturday slot but is officially the first thing to trim in a week that slips. Notice·Allow·Choose, gym, and reading are all untouched — reading is upgraded to your Hero Book once you choose one in Week 6.

The two-week expressive-writing overlay from the previous version has ended and is removed; Notice·Allow·Choose remains as the keeper, as planned.
