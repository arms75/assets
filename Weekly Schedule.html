About the two-week overlay

The highlighted slots are additions, not permanent changes. Sunday 18:30–19:30 and Wednesday 19:00–19:30 are the four expressive-writing sessions — they run for two weeks (Sun · Wed · Sun · Wed) and then come off the schedule entirely. After that, revert to your clean version.

The Saturday 15:15–15:30 Notice·Allow·Choose slot is the keeper — it stays after the intensive ends, tucked in just before gym so it doesn't move anything.

A couple of honest notes. Sunday's writing session sits in the hour after dinner — full and contained. Wednesday's is the tighter one at 30 minutes; if a session needs longer, either start Wednesday dinner a touch earlier or let it run into the cleanup window, which is forgiving on a light evening. And on writing nights, let before-bed reading go light or skip it — don't stack a stirred-up session on top of it.
