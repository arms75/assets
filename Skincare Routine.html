Weekly rhythm

Exfoliating toner on 3 set nights. Benzoyl peroxide slots into other nights only when you need it for spots. Weekends are barrier-recovery nights — just cleanse and moisturise.

Never the toner and benzoyl peroxide on the same night or the same patch of skin — that combination is what tips reactive skin into irritation.

Spot care

  • Glow Hub Zit Zap wand — daytime touch-ups on individual spots
  • Acnecide BP 5% — overnight on spots, on a BP night
  • One treatment per spot, not both stacked — and not over freshly toned skin

Milia (under eyes)

  • None of these products will clear existing milia — professional extraction is the reliable route
  • Keep balm and cream off the immediate under-eye area so you're not feeding new ones

If you add azelaic acid

  • The Ordinary Azelaic Acid Suspension 10% — evening treatment step
  • Run it alone for 1–2 weeks before layering with anything else
  • Can take over some of the chin/jaw work so you lean less on BP

Ground rules

  • One new product at a time, patch test first
  • Stinging or redness means scale back the frequency — don't push through
  • SPF every morning, no exceptions, while using acids or BP
